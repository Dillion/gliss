Say Hi to gliss. A new way of navigating content, from list to detail.

gliss is a sweet way of navigating from a list of articles to an article detail, and back again. Smooth and intuitive, it brings focus to the article; browse quickly through the list or expand to read it in detail.

Display splits to show article detail, heals to move back to the list

Meaningful transitions that reduce the complexity of the user interface by extending the virtual space, while connecting the article directly with further information.

Scroll quickly in the list, or scroll comprehensive information in the detail

Leaving the choice of browsing to the user.

Indicators provide hints to navigation

Pulling at the scroll boundaries of the detail view reveals a preview panel that displays previous or next article information. Beyond a set distance, directional arrows light up and releasing triggers the display of the previous or next article.

Supports scrolling through sections of content, and dynamic content length

Sections of content may contain many or no rows, and scrolling in the detail view will traverse articles across sections with no issues. Each detail page is dynamically sized to the length of the article.

Topped off with a custom refresh header for the list

The list view also showcases a simple way to add a custom refresh control.
